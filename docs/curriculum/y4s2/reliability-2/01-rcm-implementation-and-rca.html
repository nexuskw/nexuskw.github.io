RCM implementation and RCA

Running a real RCM study: team composition, facilitation discipline, and the information the analysis eats. Then root cause analysis as its operational partner — evidence preservation, causal-factor charting, and the 5-why's honest limits. The condensed example: an RCA on a repeat seal failure, from timeline to verified systemic cause.
