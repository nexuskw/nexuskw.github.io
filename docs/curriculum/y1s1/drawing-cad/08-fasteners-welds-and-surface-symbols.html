Fasteners, welds, and surface symbols

Lecture

Thread designation and fastener callouts. Weld symbols on structural drawings — the arrow side convention that everyone gets wrong once. Surface finish symbols and Ra values.

After this lesson you can answer:
  • On a weld symbol, what changes between arrow-side and other-side placement?
  • Decode M16×2: what do the numbers promise the fitter?
