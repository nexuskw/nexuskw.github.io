MFG 154 · LESSON 09 OF 11 · YEAR 2 · SEMESTER 1

Welding II, thermal cutting, and joint preparation

Resistance spot and seam welding; oxyfuel, friction, and explosion welding in outline; brazing and soldering distinguished properly from welding. Thermal cutting — oxyfuel, plasma, and laser — with kerf and heat-affected edges. Joint types, edge preparation geometry, and fit-up as the quiet variable behind weld quality.
