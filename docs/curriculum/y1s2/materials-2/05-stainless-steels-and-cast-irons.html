Stainless steels and cast irons

Lecture

Austenitic, ferritic, martensitic, and duplex stainless families; sensitization as the classic trap. Gray, ductile, and white cast irons and their fracture signatures. Materials the Gulf's process plants order by the ton.

After this lesson you can answer:
  • Why does sensitization make welded austenitic stainless vulnerable at grain boundaries?
  • Gray versus ductile iron: what changes in graphite shape and in fracture?
