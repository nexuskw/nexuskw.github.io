MAT 153 · LESSON 07 OF 11 · YEAR 1 · SEMESTER 2

Fatigue, fracture, and creep

S–N behavior and endurance limits; fracture mechanics in its simplest honest form (K, toughness, flaw size); creep regimes and life estimation. The three mechanisms behind most rotating-equipment and high-temperature failures, with fracture-surface reading as the diagnostic skill.
