Materials selection against corrosion

The selection ladder: carbon steel with corrosion allowance, coated steel, 304 and 316 stainless, duplex grades, and nickel alloys — each step priced against the environment it must survive. PREN put to work; galvanizing and aluminizing as sacrificial armor. Nonmetallics — GRP and HDPE — where metals lose the argument.
